What public data can and cannot support
Public data vs Fieldstate intelligence

OpenFootball-style sources can help with teams, fixtures, squads and scorers. They do not by themselves provide pass-option quality, pressure, value, or neural scenario modelling. Those require event, 360, tracking, video, or licensed datasets plus validation gates.
