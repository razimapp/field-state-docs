# FIELDSTATE OPTION D TERM SHEET

## Founder Optics Build Option

This term sheet is a non-binding discussion draft. It is not a legal agreement, not an offer of securities, and not legal advice. Any transaction should be documented by counsel.

## Transaction

Option D is based on Option B but is intended as a company-building route, not a double-priced IP buyout route. Investor receives a paid exclusive build and investment option. Razim retains Fieldstate IP during the option period. Razim and Vlad receive visible founder-level allocations, with vesting and economics tied to their respective contributions. Investor receives a staged path to majority ownership if additional funding milestones are completed.

## Indicative Starting Capitalisation

| Holder | Indicative Starting Allocation |
|---|---:|
| Razim | 30% |
| Vlad | 30% |
| Investor | 30% |

The starting allocation is indicative only and subject to definitive company documents, tax advice, securities advice, and counsel review.

## Investor Majority Path

If Investor funds agreed milestones, Investor may increase from 30% to a target majority position of 51%. Each step is achieved through **new shares issued by the company** at the funded milestone, diluting all existing holders pro rata. No transfer of founder-held shares is required for the majority path; if counsel instead recommends a call-option mechanism over founder shares, the tax, leaver, and vesting effects must be expressly addressed before signing.

| Stage | Investor Position | Razim Position | Vlad Position | Pool |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Start | 30.0% | 30.0% | 30.0% | 10.0% |
| MVP v2 funded and accepted | 35.0% | 27.9% | 27.9% | 9.3% |
| First paid pilot / commercial proof | 40.0% | 25.7% | 25.7% | 8.6% |
| Licensed data / model budget funded | 46.0% | 23.1% | 23.1% | 7.7% |
| Platform / neural programme funded | 51.0% | 21.0% | 21.0% | 7.0% |

The endpoint is intended to be economically close to a 21% founder position for each of Razim and Vlad, with pool top-ups (if needed for hiring) agreed jointly and priced into the relevant milestone. Any final structure should be checked for tax, securities, vesting, and dilution effects.

## IP Ownership and Transfer

- Razim owns Fieldstate IP during the option period.
- Investor receives only defined demo/pilot rights during the option period.
- If the option converts into NewCo, IP transfers or is licensed into NewCo only after founder/investor/company documents are agreed.
- Under the NewCo route, the value of the Fieldstate IP is reflected in the founder allocations, retainer, staged funding commitments, and agreed founder floors. Investor should not be charged a separate full IP buyout on top of the 51% majority path.
- If Investor instead wants an outright acquisition outside NewCo, that becomes Option C or a separately negotiated buyout route, and IP transfers only after full payment or escrow release.
- If the option is not exercised, exclusivity expires and Razim retains all rights.

## IP Transfer Sequence

| Stage | IP Position | Investor/NewCo Rights |
|---|---|---|
| Discussion / pre-signing | Razim retains all IP | No ownership or commercial rights. |
| Option period | Razim retains all IP | Paid exclusivity plus defined demo/pilot rights only. |
| NewCo documents agreed | Razim may grant NewCo an exclusive licence | Licence starts only after founder, investor, services, vesting, dilution, reserved-matter, and IP documents are agreed. |
| Initial funding received | NewCo may receive operating control under the licence | Control is limited to the agreed Fieldstate business and subject to payment and founder protections. |
| Assignment trigger satisfied | IP may be assigned to NewCo | Assignment occurs only after agreed funding, founder protections, role/retainer terms, and counsel-reviewed documents are complete. |
| Option not exercised / payments stop | Razim retains IP | Exclusivity and limited rights end, unless a narrow survival right is expressly agreed. |

The preferred protective route is licence first, assignment later. This gives NewCo enough control to operate while ensuring Razim does not transfer ownership before founder protections and funding are locked.

## Investor Protection

- Paid exclusivity during the option period.
- Staged path to majority ownership if agreed funding milestones are completed.
- Milestone review and acceptance criteria.
- Option or warrant mechanics to be documented by counsel.
- Information rights, source/code escrow, and board/observer rights to be agreed if appropriate.

## Founder Protection

- No automatic equity transfer before the relevant milestone is funded and accepted.
- No selective dilution or related-party issuance without agreed protections.
- Pre-emption rights, information rights, and reserved matters to be agreed.
- IP sale, exclusive licence, or assignment should require agreed approval mechanics.
- Background tooling and generic methods carve-out to be agreed.

## Conditions

- Counsel-reviewed option and company documents.
- Clear milestone definitions.
- Tax and securities review of any founder share transfer, warrant, SAFE, or call option.
- Treatment of option pool, contractors, later CTO, engineers, and future hires.
- Services, employment, interim CTO, or advisory agreement for Razim if he continues delivery or technical oversight.
- Treatment of leaver events and founder removal without cause.

## Non-Binding Effect

This term sheet is for discussion only. No party is bound unless and until definitive agreements are signed.
