# Sports-AI Direction Notes

## Core Direction

Advanced sports analytics is moving toward systems that combine event data, tracking data, video context, synthetic/augmented training workflows, and GPU-trained spatial-temporal models.

## Fieldstate Rule

Use these references to shape the dream product roadmap, not to claim current capability. Current product claims must stay tied to implemented features and evidence artifacts.

## Reusable Questions

- What does the user see at this stage?
- What data is required to make it real?
- What model class is appropriate?
- What validation gate prevents overclaiming?
- What should remain future-funded?

## Public-Safe Claim Language

- Safe: "This is the long-term direction for the product roadmap."
- Safe: "This requires licensed data, compute, and validation before customer claims."
- Unsafe: "Fieldstate already has computer-vision tracking and neural simulation."
- Unsafe: "Fieldstate can predict match outcomes or player movement at production accuracy today."
