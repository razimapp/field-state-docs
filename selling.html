How to talk about it

Simple words win.

The pitch should sound calm, practical, and credible. It should help the listener feel the value quickly.

Good wording

  • It helps football people compare decisions.
  • It shows the likely next phase of play.
  • It turns match data into scenario analysis.
  • It is a useful analyst tool first.

Avoid

  • Perfect prediction language.
  • Overclaiming match replay realism.
  • Talking only about betting edge.
  • Heavy model jargon in the first minute.

Investor line

Fieldstate is a football decision-intelligence product. It starts with analysts and expands toward clubs, academies, media, and platform APIs.
