# FIELDSTATE OPTION A TERM SHEET

## NewCo Founder-Led Structure

This term sheet is a non-binding discussion draft. It is not a legal agreement, not an offer of securities, and not legal advice. Any transaction should be documented by counsel.

## Parties

| Party | Description |
|---|---|
| NewCo | Proposed company formed to own and operate Fieldstate. |
| Razim Wasti | Founder/operator and current technical/product lead. |
| Vlad | Commercial relationship partner, role to be agreed. |
| Investor | Prospective investor in NewCo. |

## Transaction

The parties form or designate NewCo. Fieldstate IP is assigned or exclusively licensed into NewCo only after founder, investor, services, and IP documents are agreed.

## Indicative Economics

| Item | Indicative Term |
|---|---|
| Stage 1 funding | GBP 15k-30k for MVP v2 feasibility sprint. |
| Founder/operator retainer | Market basis GBP 10k-18k per month; offered at the discounted founder rate of GBP 6.4k per month only while Razim receives equal or substantially equivalent founder economics to the other founder-level participants, subject to agreed vesting, role, and protection terms. The discount is the founder's visible commitment to the company route. |
| Later model/data/cloud budget | Separately funded by Investor/NewCo as approved budget. Not included in Stage 1 funding. |
| Investor instrument | Equity, SAFE, convertible, or other instrument to be agreed. |
| Option/employee pool | 10%-15% indicative pool for CTO, engineers, ML contractors, advisors, and hires. |

## Indicative Cap Table Example

This example is included to make the commercial structure concrete. It is not a proposed final allocation and should be reviewed by counsel, tax advisers, and the parties before use.

| Holder | Indicative Range | Rationale |
|---|---:|---|
| Razim Wasti | 30%-45% | Originator of the current build, technical/product lead, and delivery operator. |
| Vlad | 10%-30% | Commercial relationship partner, role and future contribution to be agreed. |
| Investor | 25%-45% | Capital provider for MVP v2, company formation, commercialisation, and future funding. |
| Option/employee pool | 10%-15% | Future CTO, engineering, ML, data, advisory, and hiring needs. |

The ranges are alternatives, not additive; any agreed allocation must total 100%. One
worked example within the ranges: Razim 40% / Vlad 15% / Investor 33% / Pool 12%.

## Funding Ramp

| Stage | Investor Funding | Commercial Effect |
|---|---:|---|
| MVP v2 feasibility | GBP 15k-30k plus retainer | Funds current sprint and preserves founder-led structure. |
| Pilot-ready product | GBP 50k-150k additional budget | Expands company value, validates customer/pilot use, and may justify revised investment terms. |
| Licensed data / advanced model programme | GBP 250k-750k+ additional budget | Funds the higher-risk model/data stage and may be tied to additional investor rights by agreement. |
| Platform scale-up | To be agreed | Larger platform, hiring, and commercial rollout budget; cap table impact to be agreed before funding. |

## Founder Technical Role / Interim CTO

| Item | Indicative Term |
|---|---|
| Initial role | Razim acts as founding technical lead / interim CTO during MVP v2 and transition unless otherwise agreed. |
| Scope | Product architecture, engineering direction, model-lab roadmap, data pipeline oversight, evaluation discipline, technical documentation, contractor supervision, and technical investor/pilot support. |
| Authority | Technical roadmap, architecture, model-evidence standards, and contractor/hiring recommendations should sit with the interim CTO role, subject to agreed budget and board/reserved matters. |
| Retainer | Included in the founder/operator retainer while Razim actively performs the interim CTO role. |
| Later CTO hire | A future CTO may be hired by NewCo, but replacement or transition should not remove Razim's founder economics, vested rights, IP protections, or agreed advisory/transition role. |
| Handover | Any handover to a later CTO or engineering team should be documented, paid if outside normal retainer scope, and tied to clear deliverables. |

## IP Ownership

- Prior to completion, Razim retains ownership of Fieldstate IP unless otherwise agreed in writing.
- On completion, Fieldstate IP is assigned or licensed into NewCo.
- IP should be transferred to NewCo, not to an individual.
- Background tooling, generic methods, and third-party dependencies require counsel review and carve-outs.

## Founder and Investor Protections

- Founder vesting, leaver treatment, information rights, reserved matters, and pre-emption rights to be agreed.
- IP sale, exclusive licence, or material change of business should require agreed consent or reserved-matter approval.
- Removal without cause should not permit the company to capture all contribution value without agreed founder protection.

## Conditions

- Company formation and shareholder documentation.
- IP assignment/licence documentation.
- Services, employment, interim CTO, or advisory agreement for Razim if he continues delivery or technical oversight.
- Treatment of Vlad's role and commercial contribution.
- Counsel review of open-data, third-party, and future data-licence obligations.

## Non-Binding Effect

This term sheet is for discussion only. No party is bound unless and until definitive agreements are signed.
