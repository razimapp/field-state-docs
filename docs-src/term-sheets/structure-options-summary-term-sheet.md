# FIELDSTATE STRUCTURE OPTIONS SUMMARY TERM SHEET

## Status

This term sheet is a non-binding discussion draft. It is not a legal agreement, not an offer of securities, and not legal advice. Any transaction should be documented by counsel.

## Parties

| Party | Description |
|---|---|
| Fieldstate / NewCo | Proposed company or vehicle for the Fieldstate business. |
| Razim Wasti | Founder/operator and current builder of the Fieldstate product and technical assets. |
| Vlad | Commercial relationship partner and prospective commercial/founder participant. |
| Investor | Prospective funder/investor/acquirer. |

## Available Structures

| Option | Instrument | Primary Use | IP Position | Investor Path |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| A | NewCo Founder-Led Term Sheet | Company formation and founder-led venture | IP assigned/licensed into NewCo after founder/investor documents | Equity or investment instrument in NewCo |
| B | Exclusive Build and Investment Option | Paid first right while terms mature | Razim retains IP until option exercise | Option to invest/acquire on agreed terms |
| C | Full IP Assignment Term Sheet | Asset acquisition / work-for-hire-style ownership | IP transfers after full payment or escrow release | Full ownership after closing |
| D | Founder Optics Build Option | Company route with explicit Razim/Vlad founder optics and staged investor majority path | IP assigned/licensed into NewCo after founder/investor documents; no separate full IP buyout unless Investor switches to acquisition route | Path to 51% investor position after funded milestones |

## Common Exclusions

Unless expressly included in the selected definitive documents, all options exclude:

- Licensed football data costs.
- Advanced neural model training, GPU campaigns, and specialist ML contractors.
- Legal, tax, accounting, company formation, and diligence fees.
- Production infrastructure beyond the agreed sprint scope.
- Any guarantee of model accuracy, revenue, pilot conversion, or club adoption.

## Common Conditions

- No IP assignment occurs before the relevant payment, escrow release, or NewCo document completion.
- Under Option D, the NewCo route should not also carry a separate full IP buyout price; that applies only if Investor elects an outright acquisition route.
- Any exclusivity or purchase option is time-limited and paid.
- Any equity, warrant, SAFE, convertible, or share-transfer mechanism is subject to counsel drafting.
- Any commercial use before full transfer is limited to agreed demo or pilot rights.
- Background tooling, generic methods, third-party dependencies, and open-data licence obligations must be reviewed by counsel.

## Recommended Use

The parties should select one commercial structure before negotiating detailed documents. If the Investor wants greater control or lower founder equity, the corresponding cash, funding, or milestone obligations should increase.
