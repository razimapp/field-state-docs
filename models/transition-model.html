Sequence model

The transition model predicts what comes next.

It is the bridge between a changed action and the rest of the simulated possession.

Overview Models Data Roadmap

How it works

Learn the probability of the next event type, next location, and whether possession stays alive.

Where it helps

Pass to carry to shot chains, turnover risk, pressure response, and possession continuation.

Scenario fit

This is the core of the what-if engine because it turns one edit into a likely follow-up sequence.

How to test it

Use hold-out event sequences. Check top-1 accuracy, sequence plausibility, and calibration by state.

What it misses

It needs good state features. Without them, it may predict generic football instead of the specific game.

Investor wording

“This model tells us the most likely next football action after a decision changes.”
