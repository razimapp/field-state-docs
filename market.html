Who pays

Start with the people who already work with football data.

The early market is small but practical. The product should earn trust before it tries to scale.

Independent analysts

Need fast scenario explanation and clear output for clients.

Academies

Need player decision teaching and simple tactical feedback.

Lower-tier clubs

Need useful insights without enterprise complexity.

Content creators

Need strong football stories and visual explanations.

Professional clubs

Need better scenario planning and recruitment fit.

Media and broadcasters

Need live story layers and decision-based analysis.

Revenue path

The simplest path is services first, then software. Early reports can fund the product. Later, the product can move into subscriptions, licences, and API usage.

Reports

Custom tactical analysis for early customers.

Subscriptions

Analyst and academy access on a monthly basis.

Licences

Club and enterprise access by seat or team.

API

Simulation calls for platforms and integrations.
