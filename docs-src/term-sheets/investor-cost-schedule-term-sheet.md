# FIELDSTATE INVESTOR COST SCHEDULE

## Non-binding Discussion Draft

This schedule is a non-binding discussion draft. It is not a legal agreement, not an offer of securities, and not legal advice. Subject to counsel review before signature. Any transaction should be documented by counsel.

## Purpose

This schedule summarises the categories of cost an investor may fund during the Fieldstate option, MVP, pilot, data, model, and platform stages. All figures are indicative planning ranges only and exclude VAT/tax unless expressly stated.

## Parties

| Party | Description |
|---|---|
| Fieldstate / NewCo | Proposed company or vehicle for the Fieldstate business. |
| Razim Wasti | Founder/operator and current technical/product lead. |
| Vlad | Commercial relationship partner, role to be agreed. |
| Investor | Prospective funder/investor/acquirer. |

## Cost Principles

- Retainer payments fund active delivery time and do not by themselves transfer IP ownership.
- Option fees fund exclusivity or first-right mechanics and may be credited only if expressly agreed.
- Sprint/build fees fund defined deliverables and acceptance criteria.
- IP transfer, company equity, warrants, SAFE conversion, or acquisition rights require separate definitive documents.
- Licensed data, GPU training, legal fees, tax, accounting, and production infrastructure are separate budget lines unless expressly included.

## Excluded Unless Expressly Included

- VAT, taxes, bank fees, foreign exchange, or withholding.
- Legal, tax, accounting, company secretarial, and due diligence fees.
- Paid/licensed datasets beyond the stated scope.
- GPU compute beyond the stated budget.
- Third-party APIs, data providers, cloud overages, and enterprise security requirements.
- Travel, meetings, pitch materials, and customer onboarding expenses.
- Guarantees of revenue, customer conversion, model accuracy, or future fundraising.

## Payment and Control Notes

- Retainers should be paid monthly in advance.
- Build work should pause if payments are missed.
- IP transfer should not occur before the relevant payment, escrow release, or company completion event.
- Any exclusivity should be paid, time-limited, and terminate if the option expires or payments stop.
- Any staged investor ownership increase should correspond to actual funded milestones and counsel-reviewed documents.

## Non-Binding Effect

This schedule is for discussion only. No party is bound unless and until definitive agreements are signed.
