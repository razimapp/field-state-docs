# FIELDSTATE OPTION C TERM SHEET

## Full IP Assignment

This term sheet is a non-binding discussion draft. It is not a legal agreement, not an offer of securities, and not legal advice. Any transaction should be documented by counsel.

## Transaction

Investor or an agreed company vehicle acquires the defined Fieldstate IP after full payment, escrow release, or completion of agreed closing conditions. If Razim continues after transfer, delivery is covered by a separate services or employment arrangement.

## Indicative Terms

| Item | Indicative Term |
|---|---|
| Assignment price | GBP 300k-750k+ for current build plus MVP v2 path, depending on scope and rights. |
| Founder/operator retainer | GBP 12k-25k per month if Razim remains delivery lead. |
| Escrow/deposit | To be agreed. |
| Optional equity/warrants | 0%-10%, depending on cash price and ongoing role. |
| Handover scope | Source, documentation, evidence artifacts, and operating instructions as defined. |
| Excluded costs | Advanced data, neural models, legal, tax, accounting, and production operations unless agreed. |

## Assignment Trigger

No assignment is effective until the agreed payment, escrow, and closing conditions are satisfied. Assignment documents may be prepared in advance but should not become effective before the trigger.

## Assigned Assets

The assigned assets should be defined precisely in counsel-drafted documents. Background tooling, generic methods, personal know-how, third-party dependencies, and open-data obligations require explicit treatment.

## Investor Rights

On completion, Investor or the agreed vehicle receives the defined Fieldstate IP rights. Any warranties, limitations of liability, transition assistance, and post-transfer support should be separately documented.

## Razim Rights and Exclusions

- No broad non-compete beyond the defined Fieldstate scope.
- Background tooling and reusable methods carve-out to be agreed.
- No transfer before full payment or escrow release.
- No obligation to provide future services unless separately contracted.

## Conditions

- Counsel-reviewed IP assignment agreement.
- Payment or escrow mechanics.
- Handover checklist.
- Third-party licence and open-data review.
- Separate services agreement if Razim remains involved.

## Non-Binding Effect

This term sheet is for discussion only. No party is bound unless and until definitive agreements are signed.
