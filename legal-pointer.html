Keep the public story clear and the detailed legal work private.

Fieldstate can discuss product scope, delivery routes and high-level rights considerations publicly. Detailed ownership analysis, contractual drafting and negotiation positions should be handled separately with qualified advisers.

Public-safe principles

Scope

Define what funding purchases

A funded sprint, pilot, venture investment and broader rights transaction are different arrangements and should not be treated as interchangeable.

Rights

Make permitted use explicit

Agreements should state which deliverables, code, data, documentation and usage rights are included.

Review

Use professional advice

Ownership, assignment, licensing, data rights, confidentiality and investment documents require appropriate legal review before signature.

Issues to resolve before funded delivery

IssuePublic questionRequired control
Delivery routeIs the work a funded scope, pilot, venture build or another agreed arrangement?Use a written agreement that matches the chosen route.
Background and new workWhat existed before funding, and what will be created during the engagement?Describe both categories clearly without relying on assumption.
Usage rightsWho may use, demonstrate, modify, license or commercialise each deliverable?State permissions, restrictions and handover obligations expressly.
Data rightsWhich open, sample, licensed or supplied datasets may be used, and for what purpose?Record source terms and keep restricted data out of public artifacts.
Confidentiality and publicationWhich materials may be public, shared under confidence or kept private?Separate repositories and review public releases before publication.

Public documentation rule

Appropriate for public pages

High-level product descriptions, staged capability, public-safe delivery routes, evidence boundaries and a statement that agreements require professional review.

Keep outside public pages

Negotiation limits, draft clauses, confidential correspondence, detailed ownership arguments, pricing strategy and private adviser instructions.

Practical next step

Before funded work begins, agree the delivery route and obtain review of scope, rights, confidentiality, data permissions and handover terms.
