What it can do

One engine, many football questions.

The same core system can support tactical review, player decision training, recruitment, and score prediction. The first step is to keep the scope narrow.

Analysts

What should have happened next?

Compare the original action with a safer or more aggressive choice.

Coaches

How do we attack this shape?

Test pressing, build-up, and transition scenarios against opponent structures.

Academies

What was the better decision?

Use scenario replay to teach players how choices affect risk and reward.

Recruitment

Will this player fit our style?

Test how a player changes build-up, chance creation, and turnover risk.

Media

What if that pass had worked?

Turn live moments into clear alternate-outcome stories.

Prediction

What is the likely next phase?

Score prediction and live probabilities can sit beside the scenario engine.
